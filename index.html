Our Story

Nestled in the vibrant vicinity of Washington Square Park, The Arch Bar emerges as a culinary confluence of Chinese and American gastronomy. This establishment is the brainchild of a visionary group of NYU students, who sought to create a sophisticated yet accessible dining haven for their peers. The Arch Bar is not merely a restaurant; it is a celebration of diverse culinary traditions, a tribute to the dynamic student spirit, and a symbol of cultural fusion. Here, each dish tells a story, each flavor carries the legacy of its origin, and every visit is an immersion into a rich tapestry of gastronomic excellence.

Our Mission

The Arch Bar transcends the boundaries of ordinary dining. We endeavor to create an ambience that blends culinary finesse with an air of academic intellectuality. Catering to the discerning palates of NYU students and the local community, we offer a repertoire of dishes that marry the complexity of Chinese flavors with the simplicity of American cuisine. Our focus is not only on satiating appetites but also on providing a space for intellectual exchanges and the fostering of bonds over meticulously crafted meals. The Arch Bar stands as a beacon of culinary innovation and social convergence.

Our Values

At the core of The Arch Bar lies a commitment to three cardinal principles: culinary diversity, impeccable quality, and the nurturing of community. Diversity is celebrated through our menu, a curated blend of Eastern and Western culinary philosophies. Quality is the cornerstone of our culinary creations, with each ingredient chosen for its freshness and each dish executed with precision. Community is the heart of our ethos - we strive to be more than a dining destination. We aspire to be a sanctuary where ideas and cultures mingle, where the ambiance resonates with the vibrancy of university life, and where every meal is an enriching experience. The Arch Bar is a gastronomic haven where food, culture, and community are harmoniously intertwined.
